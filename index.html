About This Explorer

The Iroko Ewé Knowledge Explorer provides a public, read-only interface for navigating plant records modeled using the Iroko Framework. Data presented here was generated through OpenRefine RDF Transform and structured as linked data.

This explorer demonstrates a Sacred Metadata approach: botanical and linguistic knowledge is made discoverable while spiritually sensitive knowledge remains governed through explicit access tiers.

The dataset follows the CARE Principles for Indigenous Data Governance: Collective Benefit, Authority to Control, Responsibility, and Ethics. Community sovereignty over knowledge remains central.
